Shipping Origin

Orders are shipped directly from the United States.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days after payment is confirmed.

Processing time excludes weekends and holidays and may be affected by unexpected delays caused by weather, carrier disruptions, or other force majeure events.

Shipping Methods

Standard Shipping

Estimated delivery time: 5-7 business days after the order has been processed.

No additional fees apply other than the standard shipping fee displayed at checkout.

Express Shipping

Estimated delivery time: 3-5 business days after the order has been processed.

Express shipping may include an additional fee, which will be clearly displayed before payment confirmation.
