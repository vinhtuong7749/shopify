Return / Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a return or refund, your request must meet all of the following conditions:

  • The request must be submitted within 7 days from the date the order is marked as delivered.
  • The product must be intact, unused, and free from damage caused by the buyer.
  • Original packaging, labels, accessories, and included gifts must be kept.
  • The buyer must provide the order number and relevant proof, such as photos, videos, or invoice.
  • The product must not fall under non-returnable categories.

Requests that do not meet these conditions may be rejected.

Buyer Error / Change of Mind

This applies when the buyer requests a return for personal reasons, such as ordering the wrong product, size, or color, changing their mind, or no longer needing the item.

The buyer is responsible for two-way shipping fees:

Shipping direction Fee
Return shipping: Buyer to Seller $5.45
Reshipping: Seller to Buyer, if an exchange is requested $5.45
Total shipping fee $10.90

This amount will be deducted directly from the refund. The remaining balance will be refunded after the process is completed.

Seller Error

This applies when the issue is caused by the seller, such as wrong product, wrong size/color, technical defect, shipping damage, or product not matching the description.

Replacement

The buyer pays no return or replacement shipping fees. Replacement orders are shipped using the standard shipping timeframe from the date the request is confirmed.

Refund

Refunds are issued for 100% of the amount paid, with no shipping fees or other fees deducted.

Process

  1. Take clear photos or videos showing the product issue.
  2. Contact us within 7 days from the date of receiving the item, including proof and your order number.
  3. Our support team will review and respond within 1-2 business days.
  4. Once approved, choose your preferred resolution: replacement or refund.
  5. Follow the instructions provided by our support team.

The buyer must provide complete information and evidence, and the return must be officially approved before any item is sent back. Returns sent without prior confirmation will not be processed.

Order Cancellation

Cancellation requests are only accepted if the order has not yet been processed, packed, or handed over to the shipping carrier, and all cancellations require official approval from the seller.

  1. Contact us as soon as possible after placing your order.
  2. Provide your order number and reason for cancellation.
  3. Wait for official approval and confirmation from our support team.
  4. If approved, the order will be canceled and payment will be refunded within 3-7 business days.

Orders that have already been processed or are in transit cannot be canceled. In that case, the return/refund policy will apply depending on the situation.

Non-Returnable Cases

  1. The request is submitted more than 7 days after delivery.
  2. The product has been used, damaged, scratched, or is missing accessories due to buyer fault.
  3. Valid proof cannot be provided for seller-error claims.
  4. The product has no invoice, order number, or verification information.
  5. The product is a special promotional item or personalized product, if stated on the product page.
  6. The buyer sends the item back without official confirmation from us.

Refund Method & Time

Refund method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the order.

Refund time

Refunds are processed within 3-7 business days after the request is approved and the returned item, if applicable, has been inspected. Actual timing may depend on the bank or payment provider.
